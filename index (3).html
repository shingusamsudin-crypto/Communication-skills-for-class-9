What is Communication?

Communication is the process of exchanging information, ideas, thoughts, feelings, and emotions between two or more people through speaking, writing, or using other mediums.

The word "Communication" comes from the Latin word "Communicare" which means "to share" or "to make common".

üì§ Sender

üí≠ Message

üì° Channel

üì• Receiver

üîÑ Feedback

üåê

üòü

Emotional Barriers

üîä

Physical Barriers

üé≠

Cultural Barriers

üß†

Chapter 5

Ways to Overcome Communication Barriers

1

Use Simple and Clear Language

Avoid jargon and complex words. Speak clearly and concisely.

2

Active Listening

Pay full attention, show interest, and respond appropriately.

3

Give and Seek Feedback

Ask questions and confirm understanding to ensure clarity.

4

Be Aware of Non-Verbal Cues

Maintain eye contact, use appropriate gestures and expressions.

5

Choose the Right Medium

Select the appropriate channel based on the message and audience.

6

Be Empathetic

Try to understand others' perspectives and feelings.

7

Reduce Noise and Distractions

Find a quiet environment and minimize interruptions.

8

Control Emotions

Stay calm and composed during conversations.
